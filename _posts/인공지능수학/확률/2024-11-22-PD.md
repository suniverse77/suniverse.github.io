## Probability Density Function (PDF)

$$
P(a\leq X\leq b)=\int_a^bf_X(x)dx~,~\bigg(\int_{-\infty}^\infty f_X(x)dx=1\bigg)
$$

**Joint PDF**

$$
P(a\leq X\leq b,c\leq Y\leq d)=
\int_c^d\int_a^b f(x,y)dxdy
$$

**Marginal PDF**

$$
f_X(x)=\int_{-\infin}^\infin f_{X,Y}(x,y)dy
$$
